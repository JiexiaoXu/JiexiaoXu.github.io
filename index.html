Jiexiao Xu

I am a combined B.S./M.S. student in Computer Science at the University of Washington, broadly interested in computer systems and computer graphics.

My current work studies end-to-end SLO-aware scheduling in microservices. I have also worked on application-level crash-consistency testing and rendering-pipeline optimizations for 3D Gaussian Splatting.
