Jiexiao Xu

I am a combined B.S./M.S. student in Computer Science at the University of Washington and an incoming Ph.D. student at the University of Pennsylvania. My work sits at the intersection of computer systems and graphics.

I currently work on end-to-end SLO-aware scheduling for microservices, with a focus on using deadlines and downstream workload estimates to improve goodput under overload. I have also worked on application-level crash-consistency testing and rendering-pipeline optimizations for 3D Gaussian Splatting.

I have been fortunate to collaborate with Shihang Li, Simon Peter, Ratul Mahajan, Gilbert Bernstein, Yile Gu, and Baris Kasikci.
